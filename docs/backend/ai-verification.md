# AI Verification System

Cointribute's AI-powered verification system revolutionizes charity vetting by providing automated, transparent, and objective assessment of charitable organizations.

## Overview

Our AI verification system uses OpenAI's GPT-4 Turbo to analyze and score charities based on multiple factors, ensuring only legitimate organizations are approved on the platform.

## Technical Specifications

### Infrastructure
- **Runtime**: Node.js v18.19.0+
- **Framework**: Express.js
- **AI Model**: OpenAI GPT-4 Turbo
- **Blockchain**: ethers.js v6
- **Port**: 3001
- **Average Processing**: 20 seconds

## Verification Process

### Scoring Criteria (Total: 100 points)

**Legitimacy Check (40 points)**
- Legal registration status
- 501(c)(3) documentation validity
- Organizational structure
- Compliance history

**Impact Potential (30 points)**
- Clear mission statement
- Measurable goals
- Past achievements
- Community engagement

**Transparency (20 points)**
- Financial reporting
- Use of funds disclosure
- Governance structure
- Public accountability

**Online Presence (10 points)**
- Website quality
- Social media activity
- Media coverage
- Community feedback

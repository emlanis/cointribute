# Quick Start

Get started with Cointribute in just a few minutes. This guide will walk you through the basic steps to begin using our platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:
- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Rainbow, or WalletConnect-compatible wallet)
- Base Sepolia ETH for gas fees
- Optional: USDC on Base Sepolia for USDC donations

## Platform Access

### Web Application
Access Cointribute at: [https://cointribute.xyz](https://cointribute.xyz)

### Supported Networks
- **Network**: Base Sepolia Testnet
- **Chain ID**: 84532
- **RPC URL**: https://sepolia.base.org

## Getting Test Tokens

### Base Sepolia ETH
1. Visit the [Coinbase Faucet](https://www.coinbase.com/faucets/base-ethereum-goerli-faucet)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Request test ETH (usually 0.1 ETH per request)

## Quick Actions

### 1. Connect Your Wallet
Click "Connect Wallet" button on the homepage and select your preferred wallet provider.

### 2. Make Your First Donation
1. Browse available charities on the platform
2. Select a charity to support
3. Choose donation amount and currency (ETH or USDC)
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
5. Receive VIBE tokens and Impact NFT (if eligible)

### 3. View Your Impact
- **VIBE Balance**: Check your rewards in the wallet section
- **Impact NFTs**: View your collection in the profile page
- **Donation History**: Track all your contributions
